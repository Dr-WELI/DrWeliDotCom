Turn ideas into experiences.

Science × Art workshops help individuals and teams translate complex ideas into engaging, culturally resonant experiences.

Rather than keeping disciplines separate, these sessions explore how research, storytelling, music, movement, and visual thinking can work together — either to build something new or to learn how to create across fields.

Build something together

Two ways to work

Build

  • Create a performance, piece, or experience together
  • Translate research into music, movement, or storytelling
  • Develop outputs for events, festivals, or public engagement

Learn

  • Understand the creative process behind science × art work
  • Develop tools for storytelling and communication
  • Apply frameworks to participants’ own research or practice

Examples of work

These projects demonstrate how scientific ideas can be transformed into cultural experiences that connect with audiences beyond traditional formats.

  • Kangaroo Time — translating animal behaviour research into music, dance, and storytelling
  • G.O.A.T. (Cabra das Cabras) — bringing Brazilian animal behaviour research into cultural performance

Many of these projects sit within WildWooHoo — a platform for creating interconnected stories between nature, culture, and people through music, film, and science communication.

What this develops

  • ✔ Creative thinking and idea generation
  • ✔ Communication across disciplines and audiences
  • ✔ Ability to translate complex ideas into accessible forms
  • ✔ Confidence in combining multiple skills and identities
