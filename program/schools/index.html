Four core themes — drawn from ecology, music, and embodied learning. Adaptable by year level.

T.01

Kangaroo Time

Learning about kangaroos through music, dance, and embodied science. Flagship session adapted for primary and lower-secondary year levels.

Turning science facts into songs, movement, and short performances students can perform back to their school community.

Eco-Rhythm Lab

Ecosystems, adaptation, and animal behaviour explored through rhythm-based group activities — curriculum-linked, classroom-ready.

Helping students share science in their own voice — inquiry-to-story pathways that build confidence and presence.
