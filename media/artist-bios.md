# WELI — Platform Bios

## Spotify Bio (artist profile)

WELI is a Brazilian-Australian artist based in Sydney, creating **global pop** with Latin roots, electronic textures, and performance at its core.  
His work bridges club energy, storytelling, and cultural identity—built to move between mainstream pop spaces and bold creative experimentation without feeling forced.

Think genre-fluid by design: Latin pulse, pop precision, electronic bite, and unexpected detours that still land as one world.
The glue is WELI’s quirky personality, confident delivery, and strong production design.

Beyond music, WELI is also known for science-culture storytelling, with a PhD from The Australian National University and international recognition through **Kangaroo Time**, winner of the 2024 *Dance Your PhD* competition.  
As a Latin queer immigrant artist in Australia, he builds music that celebrates movement, difference, and belonging.

WELI is also the face and voice of **WildWooHoo**, a separate nature-science audio-visual project focused on social impact.
